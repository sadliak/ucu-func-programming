Transform the given input Seq[String] into Seq[Int]

For example:

List("1", "2", "3") should be transformed into List(1, 2, 3)

Also, consider the possibility that the given Seq[String] may have non digital values. In such a scenario, your transformation SHOULD NOT fail.

For example:

List("1", "abc", "2", "3") should be transformed into List(1, 2, 3)

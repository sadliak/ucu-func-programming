You are given with sequence of words. Take words until a word in a sequence ends with a line separator \n

For example:

List("Hello", "world!", "\n", "Test", "asda") should be transformed into List("Hello", "world!")

Please note, List("Hello", "world!\n", "Test", "asda") should be transformed into List("Hello", "world!") as well as
List("Hello", "world!\nTest", "asda") should be transformed into List("Hello", "world!")
